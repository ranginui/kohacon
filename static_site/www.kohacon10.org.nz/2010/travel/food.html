Places Nearby

The following is a non exhaustive, non authoritative list of cafes and places to eat near the Town Hall

Civic Cafe and Bar - Cafe

There is a cafe in the foyer of the Michael Fowler Centre next to the Town Hall. Head straight out the Town Hall and into the building opposite, the cafe is on the far side of the foyer.

Felix - Cafe

At the bottom of Cuba St, directly across the road from the Town Hall. Be aware they don't accept credit cards for small value purchases

FINC - Cafe

On Wakefield St, opposite the Town Hall. Walk out of the Town Hall and turn right and it is on the opposite side of the road

Nui - Cafe

On Wakefield St, under the Council Buildings. Turn right and stay on the same side of the road as the Town Hall. Pass the entrance to the City Council. Nui is in the same location as the Wellington Tourism Information Centre

Clark's - Cafe

Exit the Town Hall and turn left. Cross Civic Square and climb the stairs to the Wellington Public Library. Clark's has CafeNET Wifi and a selection of counter food.

Manners Mall - Cafes and Fast Food

Exit the Town Hall and turn right, cross the road and head up Cuba St. Manners Mall is currently full of construction equipment but is also home to kebabs, convience stores, crepes, and the ubiquitous burger franchises.

Cuba St and beyond

Thai, Japanese, Malaysian, Indian, Chinese and the traditional Kiwi pie are also within an easy stroll.
